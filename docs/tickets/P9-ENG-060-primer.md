## What Is This Ticket?

Add self-explanation prompts throughout the lesson. Before confirming answers, the tutor asks: "Why do you think that?" or "Can you explain what happened when you split that block?" The student articulates their reasoning before the tutor validates. This strengthens schema construction and transfer.
