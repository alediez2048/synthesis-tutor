# ENG-061 Primer: Assessment as Learning Mechanism (Retrieval Practice)

**For:** New Cursor Agent session
**Project:** Fraction Quest — Interactive AI-Powered Fractions Tutor for Ages 8–12
**Phase:** Phase 9: EdTech Research-Informed Redesign
**Date:** Mar 16, 2026
**Research:** Karpicke & Roediger, 2008 — retrieval practice strengthens memory more than re-teaching. The act of retrieving is the learning.

---

## What Is This Ticket?

Reframe assessment from a checkpoint to a learning mechanism. Lean into retrieval practice: more quizzing, less re-teaching. When a student gets something wrong, prioritize retrieval attempts (try again, recall what you learned) over additional explanation. Assessment becomes part of the learning loop, not just a gate at the end.

---
